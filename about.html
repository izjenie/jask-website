About Us

We invest in resilient teams building category-defining AI and fintech products.

Vision

To catalyze a thriving technology ecosystem across Southeast Asia by backing founders who apply artificial intelligence to solve high‑impact problems in finance, health, and public infrastructure.

Mission

  • Provide founder‑friendly capital from seed to growth.
  • Offer hands‑on support across product, go‑to‑market, and regulatory strategy.
  • Leverage a deep network across government and enterprise to unlock scaled distribution.
  • Champion responsible AI principles and data stewardship.

Management

Isaac Jenie

Chief Executive Officer

Isaac Jenie is an Indonesian technology and health innovation leader who serves as Advisor to the Ministry of Health, driving the nation’s healthcare digital transformation. He has played a key role in architecting platforms that power Indonesia’s vaccination programs, telemedicine, SatuSehat, and other digital initiatives in primary care, stunting, tuberculosis, and infectious-disease management. Isaac is also part of the Indonesia Government Biobank (BGSI) team, designing genomics and bioinformatics systems that enable secure data collection, integration, and research collaboration to advance precision medicine. As CEO of JASKAPITAL, he leads a venture builder focused on fintech, AI, healthcare, and retail, and as a founding member of the Indonesia Fintech Association, he brings deep expertise across technology, policy, and entrepreneurship. He is also Co-Chairman of the National AI Healthcare Committee.

Ipung Kurnia

Chairman

Ipung Kurnia is an Indonesian business leader with over three decades of experience in retail and corporate management. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing from Simon Fraser University, Canada (1986), an MBA in Business and Finance from City University, Canada (1988), and completed the Advanced Management Program at Harvard Business School in 1999. Throughout his career, he has served as President Commissioner, President Director, and Director across several companies. He currently serves as Director of PT Mitra Sarana Purnama and President Commissioner of PT Rumah Mebel Nusantara, and is an active member of Indonesia’s leading retail associations, APRINDO and HIPPINDO.
